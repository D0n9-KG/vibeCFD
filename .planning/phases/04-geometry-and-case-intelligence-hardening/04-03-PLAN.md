# Phase 4 Plan 03: Add Dynamic Blocking, Clarification, and Researcher Approval For Ambiguous Geometry or Case Assumptions

<objective>
Implement the user-locked product semantics for Phase 4: AI may suggest, but the researcher must approve before execution.

Purpose: after `04-01` and `04-02`, the product will know far more about geometry trust and case provenance. This plan turns those signals into a concrete calculation-plan draft, adds dynamic early clarification for severe ambiguity, and blocks solver execution until the researcher confirms the plan.
Output: persisted calculation-plan draft, dynamic confirmation gating, and cockpit review actions that stay separate from post-compute scientific claim levels.
</objective>
