# Phase 3 Plan 03: Add Benchmark Comparison Logic and Integrate It into Scientific Claim-Level Decisions

<objective>
Complete SCI-03 by making benchmark-backed validation a first-class, researcher-visible decision path.

Purpose: the domain layer already contains benchmark targets and validation logic, but claim-level decisions are not yet explained as clearly and consistently as runtime recovery or result reporting. This plan turns benchmark comparison into explicit evidence, then folds it into the final claim-level contract used by the cockpit and reports.
Output: structured benchmark evidence, stronger scientific gate synthesis, and workbench/report narratives that explain why a claim is blocked, limited, or research-ready.
</objective>
